Delete Your Bible Companion Account

If you would like to delete your Bible Companion account and the personal data associated with it, you can either do so directly from inside the app or by sending us a request using the form below.

Option 1 — Delete from inside the app (fastest)

  1. Open Bible Companion on your device.
  2. Tap the Profile tab.
  3. Scroll to the Account section.
  4. Tap Delete Account and confirm.

Your account is removed immediately and all data is erased within 30 days.

Option 2 — Request deletion by email

If you no longer have the app installed, email us from the address registered to your account:

Email a deletion request

Or write to hello@brainwavex.com.au with the subject line "Account Deletion Request" and include the email address linked to your account. We will verify your identity and process the request within 30 days.

What data is deleted

When you delete your account, the following personal data is permanently erased:

  • Email address and display name
  • Account password hash
  • Bookmarks, highlights, and notes
  • Reading progress, reading streaks, and reading-plan history
  • AI assistant conversation history
  • Push-notification preferences

What is retained (and why)

For legal, tax, and fraud-prevention reasons, the following limited data may be retained for up to 7 years in anonymized form:

  • Subscription receipts and billing records (required by Australian Taxation Office)
  • Aggregated, non-identifiable analytics events
Important — this action is permanent. Once your account is deleted, your bookmarks, notes, and AI history cannot be recovered. Active subscriptions are managed by Apple App Store or Google Play — please cancel them separately in your device's app store account settings if you no longer wish to be billed.

Cancelling your subscription

Deleting your account does not automatically cancel an active subscription, because Apple and Google manage payments directly. Please cancel separately:

  • iOS: Settings → [Your Name] → Subscriptions → Bible Companion → Cancel.
  • Android: Google Play Store → Profile → Payments & subscriptions → Subscriptions → Bible Companion → Cancel.

Questions

Any questions about account deletion or your personal data? See our Privacy Policy or email hello@brainwavex.com.au.
