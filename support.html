Frequently Asked Questions

  • How do I cancel my subscription?
    Subscriptions are managed directly by Apple and Google. You can cancel your subscription at any time by going to the App Store or Google Play Store account settings on your device.
  • How do I delete my account?
    You can securely delete your account directly from within the app. Navigate to Profile → Account → Delete Account. This will permanently erase your data.
